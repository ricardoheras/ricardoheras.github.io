Research interests

  • Quantum Mechanics
    • Topological quantum mechanics. Aharonov-Bohm effect, magnetic monopoles, geometric phases, anyons, magnetic skyrmions, knotted fields in quantum theory
    • Quantum interference. Entangled states, quantum non-locality
    • Quantum aspects of waves. Bohmian mechanics, WKB approximation, semi-classical waves
    • Gravitation in quantum physics. Gravitational interference
    • Boundary effects in quantum mechanics. Chern-Simons theory
  • Classical Electrodynamics
    • Electrodynamics of interacting systems. Conservations theorems of energy, momentum, and stress of independent electromagnetic systems
    • Electromagnetic angular momentum. Decomposition theorems of electromagnetic angular momentum, boundary effects of electromagnetic angular momentum
    • Electromagnetic radiation. Liénard-Wiechert fields, radiation pressure, radiation by accelerated charges, Radiation reaction
  • Optics
    • Angular momentum of light. Orbital angular momentum, spin angular momentum, boundary effects of the angular momentum of light
    • Topological optics. Optical skyrmions, field line helicity
